> It is better to have 100 functions operate on one data structure than 10 functions on 10 data structures.

-Alan Perlis

### Atomic Data Types
- `nil`
- Numbers
  + Longs
  + Ratios `22/7`
  + BigInt, BigDecimal
- Strings `"fred"`
- Characters `\a \b \c`
- Symbols `fred`
- Keywords `:fred`
- Booleans `true false`
- Regex patterns `#"a*b"`

### Data Structures
- Lists `(1 2 3)`
- Vectors `[1 2 3]`
- Maps `{:a 1 :b 2 :c 3}`
- Sets `#{fred ethel lucy}`
