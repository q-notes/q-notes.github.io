- deps
  + [clj-http](https://github.com/dakrone/clj-http)
  + [cheshire](https://github.com/dakrone/cheshire)

```clj
(require '[clj-http.client :as http])

(http/get "https://httpbin.org/get"
          {:query-params {:foo "bar"}
           :as :json})

(http/post "https://httpbin.org/post"
           {:form-params {:foo "bar"}
            :as :json})

(http/post "https://httpbin.org/post"
           {:body "{\"foo\": \"bar\"}"
            :content-type :json
            :as :json})
```
