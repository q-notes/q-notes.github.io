## layout
- `$mod+e` splith/splitv (with `$mod+h`, `$mod+v`)
- `$mod+s` stacking
- `$mod+w` tabbed

## workspace
- `$mod+num`       go to workspace
- `$mod+Shift+num` move app to workspace

## app
- `$mod+d`       open app
- `$mod+Shift+q` close app
- `$mod+f`       full screen

## other
- `$mod+Shift+r` restart i3
- `$mod+Shift+e` exit i3
