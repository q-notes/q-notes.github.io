## path
- `~/.config/i3/config` (or cp from `/etc/i3/config`)
- `cp /etc/i3status.conf ~/.config/i3status/config`

## start app on i3 startup
- `exec [--no-startup-id] <command>`

ref https://i3wm.org/docs/userguide.html#configuring
